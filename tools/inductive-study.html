What is Inductive Bible Study?

Inductive Bible Study is a method that lets Scripture speak for itself. Instead of starting with conclusions, you start with the text and draw out its meaning.

The Three Steps:

1. Observation - Carefully examine what the text actually says. Look for facts, repeated words, contrasts, and connections.

2. Interpretation - Determine what the passage meant to its original audience. Consider context, genre, and cross-references.

3. Application - Apply the timeless truths to your specific situation today.
